# LAN mobile client notices

The generated mobile client bundles the following pinned MIT-licensed libraries:

- PeerJS (`lan-transfer/peerjs.min.js`)
- `@noble/curves` 2.2.0
- `@noble/hashes` 2.2.0
- `@noble/ciphers` 2.2.0

The Noble libraries provide X25519, HKDF-SHA256, HMAC-SHA256 and
XChaCha20-Poly1305 for protocol v2. Their license texts are retained in
`lan-transfer/THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES.txt`.
